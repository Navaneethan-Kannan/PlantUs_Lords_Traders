Salt

Pure Rock Salt is a 100% natural and unrefined mineral rich in essential trace elements like calcium, potassium, magnesium and iron. It is a healthier alternative to regular table salt, ideal for cooking, seasoning, wellness treatments, detox baths and even skin care. Its natural purity and versatility make it a popular choice for culinary and therapeutic uses
Sand Salt, on the other hand, is a raw and unrefined salt mixed with fine sand particles, primarily used for industrial applications such as de-icing, construction, water softening and traditional purposes like rituals and cleaning. While not suitable for direct consumption, its cost-effectiveness and multi-purpose nature make it a practical choice for various needs.
